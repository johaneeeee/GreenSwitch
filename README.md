GREENSWITCH - Mauritius Sustainable Supplier Platform

Connecting Sustainable Infrastructure (SDG9) with Ocean Protection (SDG14) through Climate Action (SDG13)

Code4Good Hackathon 2025 - Triple SDG Integrated Solution

The Challenge

Mauritius faces interconnected sustainability challenges:

· SDG 9 Gap: Limited sustainable infrastructure and innovation
· SDG 13 Urgency: Businesses struggle with climate action implementation
· SDG 14 Threat: Marine ecosystems at risk from land-based activities

Our Solution

GreenSwitch creates digital infrastructure to connect these three SDGs, showing how sustainable industry enables climate action that protects our oceans.

The Connection: Sustainable Infrastructure enables Climate Action that protects Our Oceans

Core Features

Intelligent Discovery

· Smart search across 35+ verified Mauritian suppliers
· SDG-based filtering (SDG9, SDG13, SDG14)
· Category browsing: Solar, Organic, Packaging, Recycling
· Instant results with professional supplier cards

Secure Business Connections

· OTP verification system protects supplier contacts
· Spam prevention while enabling genuine business relationships
· Mobile-optimized verification flow
· Demo-friendly fixed OTP for smooth presentations

AI Sustainability Assistant

· Conversational interface for natural business advice
· Triple-SDG guidance with interconnected sustainability strategies
· Business-specific recommendations for restaurants, hotels, offices, retail
· Supplier matching based on SDG impact and business needs

SDG Integration

· SDG 9 Focus: Sustainable infrastructure and innovation suppliers
· SDG 13 Action: Climate resilience and carbon reduction solutions
· SDG 14 Protection: Ocean-friendly business services
· Impact dashboard for visual SDG contribution tracking

Professional Experience

· Mobile-first design optimized for all devices
· Bootstrap 5 responsive framework
· Mauritius-focused with local addresses and business needs
· Trust system with reviews, ratings, and verification badges

Technology Stack

Frontend: ASP.NET Core MVC, Bootstrap 5, Razor Pages
Backend:C#, .NET 6, Dependency Injection
Security:Custom OTP Service, Session Management
AI:Rule-based Conversational Service
Data:In-memory Repository Pattern
SDG Integration:Tagging System, Filtering Logic

Quick Start

Prerequisites:

· .NET 6.0 SDK
· Modern web browser
· Git (for development)

Installation:
git clonehttps://github.com/_johaneeeee/GreenSwitch.git
cd GreenSwitch
dotnet restore
dotnet run

Access application at: https://localhost:7000
